CARRERA: THE CLASSIC BUT CONTEMPORARY SPORTS WATCH

Named after the gruelling Carrera Panamericana race held between 1950 and 1955, the TAG Heuer Carrera celebrates speed, motorsport and the long and successful partnership TAG Heuer has had with it over the years. The original Heuer Carrera was design by Jack Heuer himself and released in 1963.
